BUILT ON HONEST WORK
AND LASTING RESULTS

The Shower Company started with a simple idea: homeowners deserve honest work and lasting results. After years in the remodeling industry, we saw how often projects were rushed, overcharged, or handled by too many different crews. We wanted to change that.

Our team focuses only on bathrooms, and that focus shows in our results. Every remodel is planned, organized, and completed by the same skilled installers from start to finish. We don’t upsell or pressure you into extras you don’t need. We listen, explain options clearly, and build bathrooms that stay watertight, practical, and clean for years.

OUR PROCESS ›

WHAT TO EXPECT
FROM START TO FINISH

1

Free In-Home Consultation

We start by visiting your home to take measurements, gather photos, and walk through your vision in detail. This allows us to fully understand your space and goals before any work begins.

2

Quote and Approval

After your consultation, we provide a clear, detailed quote with no hidden fees. Once approved, we move forward with scheduling and ordering your materials.

3

Ordering and Scheduling

All materials are ordered specifically for your project. We then schedule your installation and assign a dedicated crew who stays on your job until it is complete.

4

Installation and Completion

Our installers focus solely on your bathroom — no bouncing between jobs. Most remodels are completed within four to six weeks from your initial call. The price we quote is the price you pay.

COST OF FAILURE

When a Bathroom Remodel Fails, Everything After Costs More

Bathroom remodels are one of the most detailed projects in a home. If they’re not done right, they can lead to stress, delays, and added expense. We’ve seen it all: leaks, uneven tile, and work left unfinished.
